Two Door Cinema Club

NOTHERN IRELAND CHARME

 

Two Door Cinema Club is a an indie-rock-band with a touch of electropop. The free irish students, Alex Trimble, Kevin Baird and Sam ´Hallidayhave founded the band in 2007 in Northern Ireland. Those guys had had the hots for music to the point that they sacrificied their education in college. With the french label kitsuné they released their debut song "Four Words to Stand On". it was beloved by critics. Even BBC has called the band the most promising newcomers of the year. 2010 was the year of their breaktrough. in the same year they realeased their debut album "Tourist History" with which they made an appearance on various games such as Fifa 11, Fifa 13, Fifa 17 and Gran Turismo 5.
